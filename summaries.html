--Steady For This--

Summaries Chapter 1-10



Chapter 1:

The first chapter introduces the main character Shaun "Growls" Thompson and his best friend Shanks. They are both teenagers who are into rap and hip hop culture. Growls is shown to be awkward and clumsy, which leads to many humorous situations throughout the novel.



Chapter 2:

In this chapter, Growls and Shanks have a PE lesson where they chat and joke around. Next, Growls talks with Tanisha, a girl he likes. They plan a date and Growls is preparing everything for the date



Chapter 3:

Now, Growls decides to go on the date with Tanisha. They meet at the cinema, but Tanisha gets gradually more and more annoyed by how the date is going, until she isnt present anymore.



Chapter 4:

Here, Growls moves on with Tanisha and instead practices his rap skills with Shanks for an upcoming rap-event. Growls starts a livestream and they start freestyling. However, both of them manage to embarrass themselves infront of the viewers, so Growls decides to end the livestream.



Chapter 5:

In chapter 5 Growls and Shanks share their miserable day after the livestream went viral. After the call, Growls goes to school without Shanks for the next days, which makes Growls sad, so he goes to talk with the teacher Mr.Rix.



Chapter 6:

In this chapter, Growls goes shopping and does daily activities, but he is still in a bad mood because he cant spend some time with Shanks. Growls found Shanks outside, but Shanks mother went in the car with Shanks and decided to drive away from Growls.



Chapter 7:

Growls finds out that Shanks is in the hospital, which makes Growls act sad and frustrated. In school, Growls kicks a chair and has to go to back to Mr.Rix to talk about his emotions. There, Growls opens up and Mr.Rix gives him some advice.



Chapter 8:

In chapter 8, Growls goes to the councellor's office to talk with Karen. Growls acts cool at first, but in the end he feels better now that he opened up even more about the situation he is in.



Chapter 9:

In chapter 9, Growls plays basketball and tries out a few shots. There he gets interrupted by a girl, Shevon, who gives him advice so he can hit his basketball shots, since he couldnt hit one shot in half an hour. For the rest of the chapter, he talks with her and asks a few questions until he goes home.



Chapter 10:

Growls is going to Shevons house after school. Then, they both go to a coffeehouse called "Costa" and ask eachother some questions. They talk about their hobbies and life and become good friends. after a little fight, they get along again and Growls brings Shevon back to her home.
